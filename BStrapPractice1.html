ALDEN RICHARDS

Alden Richards is a Filipino actor, TV host, product endorser, and singer. He gained widespread fame for his role as Alden in the hit Philippine television series "Destined to be Yours" alongside Maine Mendoza. The tandem of Alden and Maine, collectively known as "AlDub," became a cultural phenomenon and gained a massive fan following. Born Richard Reyes Faulkerson Jr. on January 2, 1992, in Quezon City, Philippines, Alden started his career in the entertainment industry as a contestant on the reality TV show "StarStruck," where he finished as the "Ultimate Hunk" of the show's fourth season. Aside from his successful acting career, Alden is also a talented singer. He has released several albums showcasing his musical abilities. Some of his popular songs include "Wish I May" and "God Gave Me You." Alden Richards is known for his versatility as an actor and his down-to-earth personality, which has endeared him to fans. His career continues to thrive in various entertainment projects, making him one of the prominent figures in the Philippine showbiz industry.
