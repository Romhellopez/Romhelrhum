FAQs

How can people find my new Page?
If someone searches for the name of your Page in the search bar, they’ll be able to find your Page. Your Page may also appear in Pages to Follow.

What is a Feed? How do I set up my Feed?
Your new Page comes with a Feed that is dedicated to your Page presence, separate from your personal profile’s Feed. Anyone with Bok book access to your Page can access and view this Feed. You can follow different Pages and profiles to curate a unique experience for your Page’s Feed.

Who can manage my business presence in the new Pages experience?
You can give Facebook access to trusted people so they can manage your Page. This is similar to an Admin role on classic Pages.
